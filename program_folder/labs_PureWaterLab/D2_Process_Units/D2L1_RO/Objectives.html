Objectives

After completion of the module, students should be able to:

  • Define in their own words:

    •  Solvent, solute, permeate, retentate, semi-permeable membrane, colligative property, dissociate, desalinate,  osmosis, reverse osmosis.

  • Understand osmosis both chemically and physically and explain what causes the driving force and the significance of osmotic pressure 

  • Understand reverse osmosis (RO) and how it differs from osmosis.  Relate how different elements such as water quality effect output

  • Understand the role of RO in manufacturing Ultrapure Water (UPW) and common RO configurations

  • Understand the different membrane materials and operating conditions and when to use each

  • Perform basic RO-related calculations including unit conversions

  • Possibly E, J, K from ABET criteria
