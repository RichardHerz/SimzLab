Introduction

Lab 2 in this division includes a prototype of a distributed dynamic simulation tool. It is "distributed" in the sense that the process may contain remote process units. Remote process units are represented on the local flowsheet by blue-colored units. The local "blue" units are proxies of the actual remote units, which are located on a remote server. Messages sent to and from the local proxy units are converted to XML text and are forwarded to and received from the remote server over the Internet. Since messages sent between the local client and the server are in XML text, the computer hardware and software at the two ends may be different.

The lab is still being developed.
