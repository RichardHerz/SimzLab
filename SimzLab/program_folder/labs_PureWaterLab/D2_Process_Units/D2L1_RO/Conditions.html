Conditions

The specifications to which an RO unit is run can depend on a number of things.  The contaminants in the initial feed water as well as the amount and quality of the product water can determine what the optimal conditions for a unit are (Millipore, 2005):

Pressure:

  • High-Pressure: normal operation pressure 40 kg/cm2 or over

  • Medium-Pressure: normal operating pressure approximately 30 kg/cm2

  • Low-Pressure: normal operating pressure approximately 15 kg/cm2

  • Ultra-low-Pressure: normal operating pressure 10 kg/cm2

  • These classifications are based on normal operating conditions, under some service conditions a high-pressure membrane can be used at low pressure and a low-pressure membrane can be used at high pressure.
